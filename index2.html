About SFSG Kennels

Quality Breeding Since 2010

At SFSG Kennels, we specialize in breeding healthy, well-tempered pitbulls with exceptional bloodlines. Our dogs are family-raised, socialized, and health-tested to ensure they become wonderful companions.

We are committed to responsible breeding practices and prioritize the health and temperament of our dogs above all else. Our puppies come with health guarantees and are up-to-date on all vaccinations.
